Fresh Homemade Salsa

Source: Isabel Eats, "Fresh Homemade Salsa." https://www.isabeleats.com/fresh-homemade-salsa-recipe/

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 jalapeño pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Chop the tomatoes, onion, jalapeño, and garlic into smaller pieces.
  2. Add all ingredients to a blender or food processor.
  3. Pulse until the salsa reaches your desired consistency.
  4. Taste and adjust salt if needed.
  5. Transfer to a bowl and let sit for a few minutes to develop flavor.
  6. Serve fresh with chips or your favorite dishes.
